Tuition Fees

Students from EU/EEA countries usually pay the legal tuition fee. Use the tuition fee calculator to estimate costs.

Application Deadlines

  • 1 May: Dutch residents without visa requirements
  • 31 May: Non-EU students outside the Netherlands requiring a visa
  • 1 August: EU/EEA students or those transferring from another Dutch institution

Required Documents

  • Passport
  • Residence permit (if applicable)
  • School diploma and grade lists (with certified translations if required)
  • Proof of language proficiency

Financial Proof for Non-EU Students

Bank statements and financial documents must be submitted, ensuring sufficient funds for tuition and living expenses.
